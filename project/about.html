Strong Coffee, Strong Roots About Our Cafe

Founded in 2060 by the Sapkota brothers, our establishment has been serving up rich coffee sourced from the best organic farmers in various regions of South and East Nepal. We are dedicated to travelling new places, finding the best coffee, and bringing back to you here in our cafe.

We guarantee that you will fall in lust with our decadent blends the moment you walk inside until you finish your last sip. Join us for your daily routine, an outing with friends, or simply just to enjoy some alone time.
